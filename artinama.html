Why Soner Klend Sabbath Hutagalung?

Hello friends, here I will give an explanation of the meaning of my name. First is Soner, why Soner? Soner is a combination of my mother and father's name, SONny and ERni. Then Klend, Klend is taken from the name of the city in East Jakarta, namely Klender, why Klend? because I was born in Klender and to be good, the letters E and R were deleted. Then Sabbath, Sabbath was taken from Saturday because I was born on Saturday (Sabbath). And the last one is Hutagalung, because my father is a Batak clan with the Hutagalung clan, so the clan descended on me.
